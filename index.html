About

It's 2021 already, where are the flying cars? I guess I'm living in the perfect time to build one.

I am venturing as a home based engineer! I studied aerospace engineering in college, and after seeing the ambition of SpaceX, and the excitement in the new space industry, I was hooked. I wanted to work in aerospace, but knew it would be a challenge getting a place to do so India. So I started doing my own projects, in the comfort of my own room with my own tools.

One of the biggest projects was where I got to be a part of a team called Garud in India. Where I was supposed to be a recovery engineer for a high powered experimental rocket, I am currently a Technical Associate at Space Development Nexus (SDNx), where I oversee the internal and external R&D activities and aligns them to the business needs and future roadmaps.
